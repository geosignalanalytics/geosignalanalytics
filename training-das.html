    <h1>DAS Data Analysis & Interpretation</h1>


    <h2>Program Introduction</h2>

    <p>

      This program focuses on the analysis and interpretation of Distributed Acoustic Sensing (DAS)

      data for subsurface monitoring and seismic applications.

    </p>


    <h2>Program Scope</h2>

    <p>

      Participants will explore DAS-specific data characteristics, processing challenges,

      and advanced analysis techniques used in research and field applications.

    </p>


    <h2>Program Topics</h2>

    <ul>

      <li>DAS acquisition principles</li>

      <li>DAS signal characteristics and noise sources</li>

      <li>Processing and denoising of DAS data</li>

      <li>Seismic and ambient noise applications</li>

      <li>Case studies from real DAS deployments</li>

    </ul>


    <h2>Target Audience</h2>

    <p>

      Researchers, engineers, and practitioners working with DAS or large-scale sensing systems.

    </p>
