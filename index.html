About Us

Geosignal Research Institute (GRI) is the academic and research branch of Geosignal Analytics LLC. We specialize in developing advanced computational tools, machine learning models, and physics-informed approaches to tackle subsurface geophysics challenges. Our work spans seismic imaging, hydrogeophysics, and induced seismicity, with a mission to integrate data science and geoscience for impactful real-world solutions.

Our Services

  • Seismic Imaging & Signal Processing
  • Hydrogeophysical Data Analysis
  • Machine Learning & Deep Learning for Geoscience
  • Subsurface Reservoir Prediction

Research & Projects

Our projects focus on integrating AI-driven workflows with traditional geophysical methods. From enhancing PcP seismic phase signals with Deep Image Prior methods to modeling Enhanced Geothermal Systems (EGS), we aim to push the boundaries of subsurface characterization.
