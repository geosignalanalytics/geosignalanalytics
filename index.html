About Us

Geosignal Research Institute (GRI) is the academic and research branch of Geosignal Analytics LLC. We specialize in developing advanced computational tools, machine learning models, and physics-informed approaches to tackle subsurface geophysics challenges. Our work spans seismic imaging, hydrogeophysics, and induced seismicity, with a mission to integrate data science and geoscience for impactful real-world solutions..
