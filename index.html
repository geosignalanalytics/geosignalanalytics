Mission

At GeoSignal Analytics, our mission is to deliver cutting-edge solutions for geophysical signal processing and Earth imaging. We empower researchers, energy companies, and environmental stakeholders with advanced tools and methodologies to extract meaningful insights from complex subsurface data. By combining deep domain expertise with modern machine learning and comprehensive geophysical data analytics, we bridge the gap between research innovation and real-world geoscience applications, and we maintain a strong commitment to transparency, reproducibility, and scientific impact.

Biography

Dr. Innocent Oboué is a geophysicist and the founder of GeoSignal Analytics. He holds a Ph.D. in Resource Exploration and Geophysics from Zhejiang University, where he specialized in advanced signal processing for subsurface imaging. His research focuses on developing innovative methods for processing and analyzing diverse geophysical datasets, including seismic, Distributed Acoustic Sensing (DAS), ambient noise, and other geophysical observations. Dr. Oboué applies machine learning, interferometry, and modern signal enhancement techniques to improve imaging of the Earth's structure from the near surface to the deep interior. His work bridges academic research and real-world geoscience applications.

Services

At GeoSignal Analytics, we offer cutting-edge scientific and technical services tailored to the needs of research institutions, energy companies, and environmental agencies. Our core services for 2025–2026 include:

1. Seismic Data Denoising & Enhancement

We apply advanced signal processing and machine learning techniques to improve the signal-to-noise ratio (SNR) in large seismic datasets, including Distributed Acoustic Sensing (DAS) and conventional geophone arrays. Our denoising pipelines preserve critical subsurface features while reducing artifacts and noise.

2. Ambient Noise Interferometry & Subsurface Imaging

We process ambient seismic data to extract empirical Green’s functions and provide high-resolution images of the Earth's subsurface. This service is ideal for passive monitoring, urban geophysics, infrastructure analysis, and environmental applications.

3. Custom Machine Learning Models for Geophysical Data

From training deep neural networks to applying unsupervised learning for pattern recognition in geophysical time series, we develop bespoke AI solutions that accelerate interpretation and improve subsurface characterization.

  • Hydrocarbon Prediction from Seismic: ML-based models for detecting hydrocarbon indicators from seismic attributes.
  • Seismic Inversion: Deep learning for structural interpretation and model-based inversion.
