Mission

GeoSignal Analytics is a scientific initiative dedicated to advancing high-resolution imaging of the Earth using modern signal processing techniques, including Distributed Acoustic Sensing (DAS), ambient noise interferometry, and machine learning. Our mission is to bridge academic excellence and applied geosciences through innovation, reproducibility, and open collaboration.

Biography

Dr. Innocent Oboue is a geophysicist and signal processing expert, currently a postdoctoral researcher at Zhejiang University. His work focuses on high-resolution Earth imaging from the surface to the deep interior using techniques such as DAS, ambient noise, and AI/ML.

Research Projects

  • Signal enhancement and imaging using DAS data
  • Deep learning for ambient noise interferometry
  • Empirical Green's function reconstruction

Online Courses & Trainings

GeoSignal Analytics offers self-paced and instructor-led courses on advanced geophysical signal processing techniques.
