People

Leadership, researchers, and collaborators driving scientific excellence at GeoSignal Research Institute.

Institute Leadership

Dr. Yapo Abole Serge Innocent Oboue

Founder & Director, GeoSignal Research Institute

Dr. Oboue is a computational geophysicist specializing in seismic imaging, signal processing, and data-driven subsurface characterization. His research focuses on the development of physics-informed and artificial intelligence methods for seismic and Distributed Acoustic Sensing (DAS) data analysis.

He has extensive experience in academic research, international collaboration, and applied geophysical problem-solving. As Founder and Director, he defines the scientific vision of the Institute and leads its research strategy, training initiatives, and global partnerships.

Affiliated Researchers and Collaborators

The Institute maintains strong collaborations with researchers from universities, national laboratories, and industry worldwide. Affiliated researchers contribute through joint projects, co-authored publications, scientific advising, and student co-supervision.

These collaborations reflect the Institute’s commitment to international cooperation, interdisciplinary research, and open scientific exchange.

Students and Early-Career Researchers

GeoSignal Research Institute actively supports the training and mentorship of graduate students, postdoctoral researchers, and early-career scientists. Trainees are integrated into ongoing research projects and benefit from close supervision, advanced computational training, and exposure to international research networks.
