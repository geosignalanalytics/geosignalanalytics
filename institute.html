About the Institute

The GeoSignal Research Institute (GRI) is the academic and R&D arm of GeoSignal Analytics LLC. It focuses on developing advanced computational, data-driven, and AI-assisted methods for imaging, monitoring, and understanding Earth’s subsurface systems.

Key Focus Areas

1. Distributed Acoustic Sensing (DAS)

Development of AI-driven DAS processing and inversion algorithms for subsurface imaging, infrastructure monitoring, and environmental sensing. Future updates will include project demos and datasets.

2. Seismic Denoising and Imaging

Research on hybrid deep learning and physics-informed models for seismic data enhancement, including TSDIP and SeisPreDL frameworks for adaptive signal recovery.

3. Hydrogeophysics & Environmental Monitoring

Using electromagnetic and seismic data to identify and monitor groundwater reservoirs, soil properties, and contaminant migration patterns.

4. Earth-AI and Intelligent Sensing

Integration of multi-modal sensing (seismic, DAS, EM, satellite) with deep learning for real-time Earth system intelligence and hazard prediction.
