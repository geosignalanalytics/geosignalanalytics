Research Funding

Competitive research grants and funding programs supporting scientific excellence, innovation, and international collaboration at the GeoSignal Research Institute.

Current and Recent Funding

National Natural Science Foundation of China (NSFC)
International Research Fund — Distributed Acoustic Sensing (DAS) | 2025–2026

One-year international research project focusing on advanced signal processing and data-driven denoising techniques for Distributed Acoustic Sensing (DAS) data, with applications to seismic monitoring, urban infrastructure, and deep subsurface imaging.

Internal Research Development Program
GeoSignal Research Institute | Ongoing

Seed funding program supporting exploratory research, methodological innovation, and early-stage projects in computational geophysics, machine learning, and applied GeoAI for subsurface characterization.

Funding Strategy

The GeoSignal Research Institute actively pursues competitive funding from national and international research agencies, foundations, and industry partners. Our funding strategy emphasizes scientific excellence, openness, and long-term impact, while supporting interdisciplinary and high-risk–high-reward research.

Priority is given to funding programs aligned with subsurface imaging, seismic monitoring and forecasting, energy transition, environmental geophysics, and sustainable resource management.

Partnership and Sponsorship Opportunities

We welcome funding partnerships with governmental agencies, international organizations, and industry stakeholders interested in supporting independent scientific research and innovation in geophysics and geoscience data analytics.

Potential sponsors may support doctoral research, postdoctoral fellowships, collaborative projects, scientific events, and advanced training programs.
