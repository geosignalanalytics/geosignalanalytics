    <section>

      <h2>Ongoing Research Projects</h2>


      <div class="project-block">

        <h3>AI-Driven Seismic and DAS Denoising</h3>

        <p>

          This project focuses on the development of physics-informed and

          data-driven algorithms for seismic and Distributed Acoustic Sensing (DAS)

          noise attenuation. Advanced deep learning architectures are combined with

          classical signal processing to enhance weak seismic phases and improve

          subsurface interpretability.

        </p>

      </div>


      <div class="project-block">

        <h3>Subsurface Imaging for Groundwater and Environmental Applications</h3>

        <p>

          The project aims to improve near-surface and shallow subsurface imaging

          using electrical resistivity tomography, seismic refraction, and

          integrated geophysical inversion. Applications include groundwater

          exploration, contamination assessment, and environmental monitoring.

        </p>

      </div>


      <div class="project-block">

        <h3>Mineral and Geothermal Resource Characterization</h3>

        <p>

          This research investigates the use of magnetometry, gravimetry,

          induced polarization, and spectral analysis to characterize mineral

          deposits and geothermal systems. Emphasis is placed on integrated

          interpretation and uncertainty-aware modeling.

        </p>

      </div>


    </section>


    <section>

      <h2>Emerging and Conceptual Projects</h2>


      <div class="project-block">

        <h3>Deep Earth Imaging and Mantle Structure Analysis</h3>

        <p>

          This emerging project explores advanced seismic imaging techniques

          for probing the deep Earth, including mantle transition zone

          discontinuities and core–mantle boundary structures using global

          and regional seismic data.

        </p>

      </div>


      <div class="project-block">

        <h3>Seismic Monitoring and Hazard Forecasting</h3>

        <p>

          The objective of this project is to develop robust monitoring and

          forecasting frameworks for seismic hazards, integrating real-time

          seismic observations, statistical modeling, and machine learning

          approaches for risk mitigation.

        </p>

      </div>


      <div class="project-block">

        <h3>Integrated Subsurface Intelligence for Energy Transition</h3>

        <p>

          This initiative targets the development of multi-physics and

          multi-scale subsurface models supporting geothermal energy,

          carbon storage, and sustainable hydrocarbon production in the

          context of global energy transition.

        </p>

      </div>


    </section>
