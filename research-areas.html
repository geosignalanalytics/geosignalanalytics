Research Areas

Fundamental and applied research in geophysics, subsurface imaging, and data-driven Earth science.

Subsurface & Near-Surface Characterization

Research in this area focuses on the characterization of near-surface and shallow subsurface structures for groundwater exploration, environmental studies, and engineering applications.

  • Groundwater exploration and aquifer characterization
  • Electrical resistivity tomography (ERT) and induced polarization
  • Near-surface seismic methods and refraction imaging
  • Environmental and engineering geophysics

Mineral & Resource Exploration Geophysics

This research area addresses the development and application of geophysical methods for mineral exploration and hard-rock characterization, with a strong emphasis on integrated and non-invasive approaches.

  • Magnetometry, gravimetry, and spectrometric methods
  • Induced polarization for mineral prospecting
  • Geophysical investigation of mining sites and granite quarries
  • Integrated interpretation for resource assessment

Seismic Imaging & Monitoring

Research activities include advanced seismic processing, imaging, and monitoring techniques aimed at understanding Earth structure and mitigating seismic risk.

  • Active and passive seismic imaging
  • Seismic monitoring and earthquake detection
  • Seismic hazard assessment and risk mitigation
  • Data-driven approaches to seismic forecasting

Energy Geophysics: Hydrocarbon & Geothermal Systems

This area focuses on subsurface imaging and monitoring for energy-related applications, including hydrocarbon exploration and geothermal systems.

  • Seismic imaging for hydrocarbon exploration
  • Reservoir characterization and monitoring
  • Geothermal system assessment
  • Multiphysics approaches for energy geoscience

Distributed Acoustic Sensing (DAS) & Large-Scale Sensing

The Institute conducts advanced research on Distributed Acoustic Sensing (DAS) and large-scale sensing technologies for seismic, environmental, and infrastructure monitoring.

  • DAS data processing and interpretation
  • Urban, environmental, and tectonic DAS applications
  • Noise attenuation and weak-signal enhancement
  • DAS-based seismic monitoring

Computational & AI-Driven Geophysics

This transversal research area develops advanced computational, signal processing, and artificial intelligence methods for geophysical data analysis and subsurface imaging.

  • Advanced signal processing for geophysical applications
  • Machine learning and deep learning in seismic processing
  • Physics-informed and data-driven modeling
  • Large-scale geophysical data analytics

Multi-Scale Earth Imaging

The Institute promotes integrated research spanning near-surface, subsurface, and deep-Earth imaging, enabling a comprehensive understanding of Earth systems across multiple spatial scales.
