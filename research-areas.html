    <section>

      <h2>Subsurface & Near-Surface Characterization</h2>

      <p>

        Research in this area focuses on the characterization of near-surface

        and shallow subsurface structures for groundwater exploration,

        environmental studies, and engineering applications.

      </p>

      <ul>

        <li>Groundwater exploration and aquifer characterization</li>

        <li>Electrical resistivity tomography (ERT) and induced polarization</li>

        <li>Near-surface seismic methods and refraction imaging</li>

        <li>Environmental and engineering geophysics</li>

      </ul>

    </section>


    <section>

      <h2>Mineral & Resource Exploration Geophysics</h2>

      <p>

        This research area addresses the development and application of

        geophysical methods for mineral exploration and hard-rock characterization,

        with a strong emphasis on integrated and non-invasive approaches.

      </p>

      <ul>

        <li>Magnetometry, gravimetry, and spectrometric methods</li>

        <li>Induced polarization for mineral prospecting</li>

        <li>Geophysical investigation of mining sites and granite quarries</li>

        <li>Integrated interpretation for resource assessment</li>

      </ul>

    </section>


    <section>

      <h2>Seismic Imaging & Monitoring</h2>

      <p>

        Research activities include advanced seismic processing, imaging,

        and monitoring techniques aimed at understanding Earth structure

        and mitigating seismic risk.

      </p>

      <ul>

        <li>Active and passive seismic imaging</li>

        <li>Seismic monitoring and earthquake detection</li>

        <li>Seismic hazard assessment and risk mitigation</li>

        <li>Data-driven approaches to seismic forecasting</li>

      </ul>

    </section>


    <section>

      <h2>Energy Geophysics: Hydrocarbon & Geothermal Systems</h2>

      <p>

        This area focuses on subsurface imaging and monitoring for energy-related

        applications, including hydrocarbon exploration and geothermal systems.

      </p>

      <ul>

        <li>Seismic imaging for hydrocarbon exploration</li>

        <li>Reservoir characterization and monitoring</li>

        <li>Geothermal system assessment</li>

        <li>Multiphysics approaches for energy geoscience</li>

      </ul>

    </section>


    <section>

      <h2>Distributed Acoustic Sensing (DAS) & Large-Scale Sensing</h2>

      <p>

        The Institute conducts advanced research on Distributed Acoustic Sensing (DAS)

        and large-scale sensing technologies for seismic, environmental,

        and infrastructure monitoring.

      </p>

      <ul>

        <li>DAS data processing and interpretation</li>

        <li>Urban, environmental, and tectonic DAS applications</li>

        <li>Noise attenuation and weak-signal enhancement</li>

        <li>DAS-based seismic monitoring</li>

      </ul>

    </section>


    <section>

      <h2>Computational & AI-Driven Geophysics</h2>

      <p>

        This transversal research area develops advanced computational,

        signal processing, and artificial intelligence methods

        for geophysical data analysis and subsurface imaging.

      </p>

      <ul>

        <li>Advanced signal processing for geophysical applications</li>

        <li>Machine learning and deep learning in seismic processing</li>

        <li>Physics-informed and data-driven modeling</li>

        <li>Large-scale geophysical data analytics</li>

      </ul>

    </section>


    <section>

      <h2>Multi-Scale Earth Imaging</h2>

      <p>

        The Institute promotes integrated research spanning

        near-surface, subsurface, and deep-Earth imaging,

        enabling a comprehensive understanding of Earth systems

        across multiple spatial scales.

      </p>

    </section>
