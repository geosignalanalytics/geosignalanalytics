Workshops, Short Courses & Certificates

Intensive training formats designed to deliver focused, hands-on expertise in geophysics, data science, and computational Earth sciences.

Program Introduction

This program gathers short-term academic and professional training activities, including workshops, focused short courses, and certificate-oriented modules delivered by the GeoSignal Research Institute.

Program Scope

The activities emphasize practical skills, methodological depth, and exposure to emerging research directions through intensive formats ranging from a few days to several weeks.

Program Topics

  • Short courses on emerging topics in geophysics and GeoAI
  • Hands-on workshops using real seismic and DAS datasets
  • Research-oriented training sessions and tutorials
  • Customized programs for universities, research centers, and industry

Courses / Teaching Materials

  • 1. Intensive Workshops
    Short-format workshops focusing on specific tools, methods, or datasets, with strong emphasis on practical implementation.
  • 2. Focused Short Courses
    Compact courses covering well-defined topics such as seismic imaging, DAS processing, or machine learning workflows.
  • 3. Research Training Modules
    Advanced modules designed for graduate students and researchers, bridging theory, computation, and applied case studies.
  • 4. Certificates & Customized Programs
    Certificate-oriented courses and tailor-made training developed in collaboration with academic or industrial partners.

Target Audience

Students, researchers, engineers, and professionals seeking targeted, intensive, and high-impact training experiences.
