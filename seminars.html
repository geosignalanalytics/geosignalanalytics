Seminars & Scientific Events

A forum for scientific exchange, interdisciplinary dialogue, and dissemination of cutting-edge research in geophysics and subsurface data science.

Institute Seminar Series

The GeoSignal Research Institute Seminar Series provides a regular platform for researchers, visiting scholars, and invited experts to present recent advances in geophysics, signal processing, and data-driven subsurface science.

Seminars are designed to foster critical discussion, promote interdisciplinary perspectives, and strengthen collaboration across academia, industry, and public research institutions.

Workshops & Special Events

In addition to regular seminars, the Institute organizes focused workshops, short courses, and thematic scientific events addressing emerging topics in geophysical research and applied GeoAI.

These events aim to support knowledge transfer, capacity building, and international collaboration among researchers and professionals.
