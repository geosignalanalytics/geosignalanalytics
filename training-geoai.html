Applied GeoAI for Subsurface Imaging

Program Introduction

This program explores the application of artificial intelligence techniques to subsurface imaging and geophysical interpretation.

Program Scope

The training emphasizes practical AI-driven workflows for imaging, inversion, and characterization of complex subsurface structures.

Program Topics

  • GeoAI concepts and workflows
  • AI-driven imaging and inversion
  • Uncertainty and model robustness
  • Hybrid physics–AI approaches
  • Applications to seismic and monitoring data

Target Audience

Advanced students, researchers, and professionals interested in AI-based subsurface analysis.
