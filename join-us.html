Join Us

Become part of an interdisciplinary research environment dedicated to advancing geophysical science, computational innovation, and responsible subsurface exploration.

Why Join GeoSignal Research Institute?

GeoSignal Research Institute offers a research-driven environment focused on methodological rigor, intellectual independence, and scientific impact. Members of the Institute engage in cutting-edge research at the interface of geophysics, signal processing, and artificial intelligence, while contributing to real-world geoscience challenges.

Opportunities

PhD Students

Doctoral candidates are welcome to pursue research in computational geophysics, seismic imaging, DAS data analysis, and AI-driven subsurface interpretation. PhD projects are designed to combine strong theoretical foundations with advanced numerical and data-driven methods.

Postdoctoral Researchers

Postdoctoral fellows work on advanced research topics aligned with the Institute’s core projects, contributing to high-impact publications, grant development, and international collaborations.

Visiting Scholars

Visiting researchers and faculty members are encouraged to collaborate on joint research initiatives, short-term projects, or sabbatical visits, fostering international scientific exchange.

Research Engineers and Data Scientists

Engineers and applied scientists contribute to algorithm development, software implementation, and large-scale data analysis supporting the Institute’s research and applied programs.

How to Apply

Prospective candidates are invited to submit a brief statement of research interests, a curriculum vitae, and relevant publications or technical portfolios. Applications are reviewed on a rolling basis.

Please contact the Institute via the Contact page or send an inquiry directly to discuss potential research alignment and collaboration opportunities.
